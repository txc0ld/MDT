# Mini Dream Time Character Bible v0.1

Status: locked for Phase 0 prototype.  
IP rule: all characters are original. Do not evoke protected characters, studios, franchises, or existing children’s properties. Keep dated prompts, specs, and assets as independent-creation records.

## Scale reference

- Daisy is the smallest walking character, about 1 unit tall.
- Itsy is slightly smaller than Daisy at 0.8 units but wider due to legs.
- Pip is about 1.6 Daisy units tall.
- Dino is about 3 Daisy units tall, large but gentle and non-threatening.

## Daisy, ladybug

**Role:** warm expressive heart of the group.  
**Personality:** brave enough to speak first, kind enough to notice everyone’s feelings.  
**Signature features:** round black head and body; red wing shells with large black spots; peach muzzle/cheeks; tiny black bead nose; two flexible black antennae with round tips; small black arms and legs; large warm brown eyes with soft highlights; rosy cheeks; small smiling mouth.  
**Palette:** shell red `#D9412E`, shell shadow `#A82018`, body black `#181312`, body highlight `#34302B`, cheek peach `#F2A68F`, eye brown `#5B351E`, eye white `#FFF7EA`.  
**Canonical expressions:** gentle smile, delighted laugh, concerned listening, sleepy contentment, brave curiosity.  
**Do not:** make her a generic beetle, add wings spread like a fairy, change spot count wildly, give human hair, add clothing, make antennae uneven or sharp.

**Locked descriptor:** Daisy is a tiny original ladybug friend, round black head and body, glossy red shell wings with large black spots, peach cheeks and tiny bead nose, two soft black antennae with round tips, small black arms and legs, huge warm brown eyes with soft highlights, kind expressive smile, about one third of Dino’s height, palette red #D9412E black #181312 peach #F2A68F brown #5B351E.

## Dino, small green dinosaur

**Role:** gentle giant, curious, brave-but-soft.  
**Personality:** big feelings, careful hands, asks honest questions, protects smaller friends without dominating them.  
**Signature features:** rounded friendly dinosaur with big head, soft snout, small rounded body, orange rounded back plates, olive green spots, cream-yellow belly, four stubby limbs, three rounded cream claws per foot, large warm brown eyes, thick soft brow ridges, broad friendly smile with small rounded teeth only when smiling.  
**Palette:** body green `#78B83E`, body shadow `#4E8E32`, spot green `#3F7D37`, belly cream `#D9C46D`, plate orange `#E46D2D`, claw cream `#F0E2B8`, eye brown `#68401F`.  
**Canonical expressions:** curious wonder, sheepish worry, delighted laugh, careful thinking, sleepy protective calm.  
**Do not:** make him fierce, spiky, reptilian-realistic, sharp-toothed, too huge for frame, or visually similar to any known dinosaur character.

**Locked descriptor:** Dino is a small original green dinosaur friend, rounded toy-like body and oversized soft snout, olive green spots, cream-yellow belly, rounded orange back plates, stubby gentle limbs with cream rounded claws, huge warm brown eyes with soft brow ridges, broad kind smile, three times Daisy’s height but non-threatening, palette green #78B83E spot green #3F7D37 belly #D9C46D orange #E46D2D.

## Pip, tiger cub

**Role:** shy newcomer growing in confidence.  
**Personality:** quiet at first, observant, thoughtful, becomes playful when included.  
**Signature features:** small plush tiger cub; oversized rounded head; amber-orange fur; soft cream muzzle, inner ears, chest, and paw tips; rounded black stripes with hand-painted irregularity; large warm brown eyes; small coral nose; fluffy cheek tufts; short striped tail with rounded dark tip.  
**Palette:** fur orange `#E99632`, fur shadow `#B86A25`, stripe brown-black `#2A1C17`, cream `#F3D6A7`, nose coral `#D96B4A`, eye brown `#6A3C1F`, inner ear pink `#E7A58E`.  
**Canonical expressions:** shy peek, hopeful smile, nervous courage, joyful surprise, sleepy comfort.  
**Do not:** make Pip adult-sized, aggressive, too realistic, too many sharp stripes, or identical to existing tiger characters.

**Locked descriptor:** Pip is a small original tiger cub friend, plush rounded amber-orange body, soft cream muzzle and chest, rounded hand-painted dark stripes, fluffy cheek tufts, small coral nose, short striped tail, huge warm brown eyes, shy hopeful posture, about half Dino’s height and taller than Daisy, palette orange #E99632 cream #F3D6A7 dark stripe #2A1C17 coral #D96B4A.

## Itsy, spider

**Role:** comic relief, makes scary things silly.  
**Personality:** theatrical, kind, harmless, loves echoes and tiny jokes.  
**Signature features:** small fuzzy charcoal-gray round spider body; eight soft noodle-like legs with rounded feet; oversized friendly eyes; tiny smile; faint silver fuzz rim; optional tiny amber belly freckles; not scary, not realistic.  
**Palette:** body charcoal `#3C3835`, body highlight `#5D5752`, fuzz silver `#A39A90`, eye warm gray `#EEE2D0`, pupil black `#181312`, tiny smile peach `#D79C83`, belly freckle amber `#B87937`.  
**Canonical expressions:** goofy grin, proud bow, mock-scary playful face, gentle reassurance, sleepy curl.  
**Do not:** anchor identity to a nursery rhyme, make web horror imagery, sharp fangs, many tiny eyes, realistic arachnid detail, or frightening shadows.

**Locked descriptor:** Itsy is a small original fuzzy spider friend, round charcoal-gray plush body, eight soft noodle-like legs with rounded feet, faint silver fuzz rim, two oversized warm friendly eyes, tiny peach smile, harmless comic posture, slightly shorter than Daisy but wider, palette charcoal #3C3835 silver #A39A90 warm eye #EEE2D0 peach #D79C83.

## Cross-character QA

A scene is rejected if any character loses its locked palette, eye shape, proportions, signature features, or relative scale; if a character looks like a different individual; if any visual text appears; if a scared beat becomes genuinely frightening; or if the scene feels too bright/interactive for bedtime.
